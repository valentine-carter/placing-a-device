Field safety notices and corrective actions

Field safety corrective actions (FSCA) and field safety notices (FSN) are an important part of post-market surveillance as set out in the Medical Devices (Post-market Surveillance Requirements) (Amendment) Regulations 2024.

Manufacturers must take field safety corrective actions (FSCA) to prevent or reduce the risk of a serious incident in relation to a device which has already been placed on the market or put into service.

Field safety notices

You should communicate details of FSCA to customers and users using a FSN.

We publish weekly lists of FSNs that affect the UK to subscribers.
