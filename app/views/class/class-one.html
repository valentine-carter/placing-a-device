Class I medical devices

Your medical device would likely be classified as a class I medical device.

This means that it is low risk device with minimal potential to cause harm, such as bandages or hand-held surgical instruments.

Each class is subject to a different conformity assessment route and requires different documentation. Make a note of your classification so that you can find out which route you will need to follow and how to arrange your conformity assessment.
