Class IIb conformity assessment route

You need to declare that your Class IIb device conforms to the requirements in UK MDR.

You also need to apply to an approved body to carry out a conformity assessment to approve your declaration.

As set out in Part II of the UK MDR, if your device falls into this category you must carry out either:

  • an audit of full quality assurance system
  • a type-examination

If you choose to carry out a type-examination you must also follow one of these procedures:

  • an examination and testing of each product or homogenous batch of products (Annex IV)
  • an audit of the production quality assurance system (Annex V)
  • an audit of final inspection and testing (Annex V)

You can place a UKCA mark on your product and place it on the market when you have received a certificate from the approved body.
