Find out if an exemption applies to your medical device

You do not need to notify us of a clinical investigation if you have manufactured the medical device in-house for your own patients with no objective to place it on the market.

However, you may need to notify the MHRA of a clinical investigation if you want to provide a medical device to another organisation, that up until now has been manufactured in-house for patients, for data to support safety and performance of a commercial product.

Exemptions do not apply to in-vitro diagnostic devices (IVDs).

You can use this service to find out if an in house or health institution exemption applies.
