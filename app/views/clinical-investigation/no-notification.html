You do not need to notify the MHRA

You do not need to notify the MHRA about your planned clinical investigation at this point in time.

If, after you have started your study, you make any changes, amendments or modifications you should re-assess whether you need to notify us of your study.

All studies intending to use the data collected in their study for conformity assessment of the medical device are required to notify MHRA, as set out in the Medical Device Regulations 2002 (as amended)

There may be other regulations which apply to your study and you must make sure you understand and fulfil your legal obligations.
