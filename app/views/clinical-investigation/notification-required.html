You need to notify the MHRA

You will need to notify the MHRA about your planned clinical investigation 60 days before it starts.

You should submit your notification using our online system. You can read our guidance about clinical investigations and how to notify us of your planned investigation.

For specific enquires relating to applications for clinical investigations, contact info@mhra.gov.uk and add ‘clinical investigation enquiry’ in the subject line.

The MHRA clinical investigations team can offer a comprehensive device regulatory advice meeting. We cannot review individual documents but we can provide guidance on navigating the regulatory landscape.
