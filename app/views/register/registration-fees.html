Registration fees: financial year ending 31 March 2027

We have replaced the current one-off registration fee regime for the 2026/27 financial year.

Manufacturers will be charged annually based on how many level 2 Global Medical Devices Nomenclature (GMDN) categories devices are registered under.

If multiple GMDN and products are registered under the one level 2 Category, you will only be charged once per year for that level 2 GMDN category. Some products fall under more than one level 2 GMDN Category, and these will also only be charged once per a year.

For registrations made within the charging period, 1 April 2026 to 31 March, 2027 the annual fee will be payable for the registration on a pro rata basis. Calculation of each fee will be based on the level 2 GMDN categories that their registrations fall under on 31 March 2026.

The estimated unit cost for the fee from 1 April 2026 will be in the region of £300 per year, per level 2 GMDN device.
